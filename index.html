Disease Identified: Leaf Spot Disease

Leaf Spot Disease is a common fungal infection causing dark circular spots, yellow halos, and reduced plant growth. It spreads quickly in warm and humid climates.

Recommended Treatment

  • Remove and destroy infected leaves.
  • Apply copper-based or broad-spectrum fungicides.
  • Improve plant spacing to enhance airflow.
  • Avoid overhead watering.
  • Sanitize gardening tools.

Prevention Tips

  • Practice crop rotation.
  • Maintain soil health.
  • Monitor crops regularly.
  • Ensure proper drainage.
  • Use resistant seed varieties.
